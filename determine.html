Determine the Goals

First step of the DECIDE Framework

Overview

In this initial step, we established clear objectives for our evaluation:

  • Identify usability issues that affect user experience
  • Evaluate the system's compliance with Nielsen's 10 Usability Heuristics
  • Determine the severity of identified issues
  • Establish a foundation for proposing improvements

These goals guided our evaluation process and helped us maintain focus on the most critical aspects of the system's usability.

Detailed Methodology

Our team conducted a thorough analysis of the system's goals and objectives. We interviewed stakeholders to understand their expectations and reviewed existing documentation to align our evaluation with the system's intended purpose.

Goal Prioritization

We prioritized our goals based on the following criteria:

  1. Impact on user experience
  2. Alignment with business objectives
  3. Technical feasibility of improvements
  4. Resource constraints

Key Considerations

When determining our evaluation goals, we considered:

  • User Diversity: Different user groups have different needs and expectations
  • System Complexity: The system has multiple components with varying levels of complexity
  • Critical Functionality: Some features are more critical to the system's purpose than others
  • Known Issues: Existing feedback and reported problems from users
